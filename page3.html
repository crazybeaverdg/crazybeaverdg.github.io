About Us

History of The Crazy Beaver Disc Golf Alliance.

Back in 2015, there were two beaver brothers, Ryan and Jim. These guys had a combined vision of making the PLAYERS the focal point of an event, along with raising money for local charities seemed to fill a niche that was missing in area tournaments. 

Their goal was to have 100% of player entry fees go straight into payouts. Sponsors would pay all PDGA and park district fees. Additionally, they would offer huge and innovative players packs, paid for by sponsors.

They decided to run a new kind of tournament with this vision - the DISContinuum Crazy Beaver Disc Golf Event at Bevier Park in Waukegan.

It was a huge success! So much so that they decided to run one every year. But Ryan and Jim wanted to take this to another level (B-tier) and decided that it would be best to do it without involving politics or other restraints, so they hooked up with a few like-minded players and formed the CRAZY BEAVER DISC GOLF ALLIANCE.

CB2 was held at Round Lake DGC in 2016 on the hottest day of the year. It still filled with even more players than their inaugural year. The charity raffle benefitted Beckham Zwier, a local boy with multiple physical disabilities. Over $400 was raised.

2017 saw the event move to the spacious Anna Page DGC in Rockford, IL. Using portions of all three courses there, CB3 saw a lot of new players from all across northern IL. With a charity raffle that benefitted the Rockford Park District Special Recreation program, they again raised over $400.

But after three years of taking the lead, the older beaver brother wanted his younger sibling to let his talents shine. So, in 2018, Ryan will take the Crazy Beaver 4 to another level. Partnering with the always-supportive Dellwood Disc Golf Pro Shop, Unstable Disc Golf Gear, and ZipperGrips, this year's event will be held at The Canyons in Lockport, IL.

The CBDGA is a free-flowing, non-membership-based organization that puts fun and fairness back into local competitive events. Players really do come first, so you can expect to have a blast every minute of the tournament.
