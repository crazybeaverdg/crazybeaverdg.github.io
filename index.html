About Us
  • NEW FORMAT - We're trying something different this year since we don't want to be boring, and we don't always want to go with the status quo! CB4 will be an official Ratings-Based PDGA event.
  • WHAT DOES THAT MEAN? -  Unlike the traditional breakdown of divisions (MA1, MA2, MA3, etc.) CB4 will be a unified field of players. That means all players regardless of age/gender will be grouped with thier similarly rated players.
  • FOR EXAMPLE -  If you are rated 935 your group can potentially have an 935 rated 80-year man, with a 930 rated woman, with a 928 rated 6-year old kid, and a 920 rated 26-year old. (Would that happen? maybe...we've never had an 80 year old play our events...yet. But it's all about fun!)
